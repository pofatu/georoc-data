# Content


`georoc.sqlite.gz` contains data from [GEOROC's precompiled datasets](http://georoc.mpch-mainz.gwdg.de/georoc/CompFiles.aspx) as listed below.
To avoid redundancy, files from the sections "Minerals", "Rocks", "Inclusions" have been excluded.

Upon loading the data into SQLite, a couple of apparent errors have been corrected.
These corrections are listed in [errata.log](errata.log).
